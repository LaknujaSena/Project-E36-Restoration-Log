# 🏎️ Project E36: Engineering & Restoration Log

Welcome to my technical build and restoration log for a **1993 BMW E36**. Rather than just a standard project car journey, this repository serves as an engineering case study. 

My focus here is on understanding automotive systems, documenting professional diagnostic and mechanical workflows, managing the supply chain for classic European parts, and implementing custom electronic enhancements.

---

## 📊 Project Specifications
- **Chassis:** BMW E36 (1993 Sedan)
- **Engine Platform:** M40B18 (1.8L Inline-4, SOHC)
- **Transmission:** Manual 5 speed
- **Project Goal:** Comprehensive mechanical restoration, reliability optimization for tropical climates, and custom electronics integration.

---

## 🛠️ My Role: Systems Engineering & Project Management

While specialized automotive workshops handle heavy machining, major structural overhauls, and advanced fabrication, my role throughout this journey involves:

1. **Diagnostics & Root Cause Analysis:** Identifying performance faults, reading early OBD-I/analog systems, and diagnosing electrical gremlins using multimeters and wiring schematics.
2. **Technical Documentation:** Studying workshop manuals, understanding component interdependencies (e.g., vacuum systems, cooling loops), and logging mechanical workflows.
3. **Supply Chain & Sourcing:** Procuring OEM and upgraded aftermarket components globally, analyzing material advantages (e.g., metal vs. plastic water pump impellers).

## 🔧 Technical Log Highlights

### ⚡ Systems Focus: Electronics & Diagnostics
* **The Challenge:** Resolving erratic idling issues common to early 90s Bosch Motronic engine management systems.
* **Investigation:** Documented the workshop testing the Idle Air Control Valve (ICV) and checking the Air Flow Meter (AFM) track for wear. Tracked down vacuum leaks in the intake boot using simple pressure testing principles.
* **Key Takeaway:** Gained deep insight into how early analog/digital hybrid electronic fuel injection systems balance sensor feedback to maintain stoichiometric air-fuel ratios.

### 🌡️ Systems Focus: Fluid Dynamics & Cooling
* **The Challenge:** Overhauling the notoriously fragile E36 cooling loop to handle high ambient temperatures.
* **Solution:** Coordinated a full cooling refresh. Documented the replacement of the stock radiator, thermostat, and water pump. 
* **Engineering Insight:** Analyzed why upgrading from a standard plastic-impeller water pump to a composite or metal-impeller variant prevents catastrophic cavitation and structural failure at high RPMs.
